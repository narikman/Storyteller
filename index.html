🌟 The Core Idea: Living Tales

Storyteller is not just an app — it's a magical companion. Kids can create their own fairy tale or choose from a rich catalog, and then listen to it with immersive voice narration. AI brings stories to life, making bedtime magical again.

🌙 What problem do we solve?

Modern parents are busier than ever — less time for reading aloud. Children end up glued to passive screens, missing the warmth of storytelling. Our goal is to bring back the emotional bond through interactive, AI-generated tales that develop imagination, language skills, and quality family moments.

🎨 Full app prototype — main screen design

⚡ Innovations & magic

  • AI-generated unique tales — each story adapts to child's age and interests.
  • Voice narration — clear, warm reading (more voices coming in future updates).
  • Interactive audio-visual mode — subtle animations while listening.
  • Bilingual tales — learn English & native languages through fairy tales.
🎙️ Currently 5 built-in voices — expanding to 20+ voices in future releases!

✨ Magical Functions

  • 📖 Create your own fairy tale — choose heroes, setting, and moral lesson.
  • 📚 Rich catalog of 10+ tales — classic & original stories.
  • 🎙️ Voice narration (more voice options coming in future updates).
  • 🤖 AI co-writer — helps parents generate personalized bedtime stories in 2 minutes.
