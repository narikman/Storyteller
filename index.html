About project

«Storyteller» - This is an app that creates a fairy tale itself, or you can choose a fairy tale and have it read to you in any voice.

Why us?

We are a team of enthusiasts who want to help children and their parents. We are ready to create a large catalog of fairy tales.

What problem are we solving?

Parents don't have time to read fairy tales to their children.That's why children spend a lot of time on their phones. They become addicted and don't develop properly. Our goal is to address this issue.

Innovations

  • AI voice acting
  • You can create your own fairy tale.
  • Adaptation for children

Functions

  • Creating a fairy tale for yourself
  • Choosing a fairy tale from catalog
  • Voice change

What we used?

  • Python
  • PySide6
  • Github
